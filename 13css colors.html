Hexadecimal red-green-blue. Can be six or three hexadecimal digits (0 to F). Using six digits, the first two specify the amount of red, second two the amount of green, and third two the amount of blue. The three-digit form is a shortened notation with the first digit red, second green, and thrid blue.

Predefined color names include aqua, black, blue, fuchsia, gray, green, lime, maroon, navy, olive, purple, red, silver, teal, white, and yellow
